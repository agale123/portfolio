PROJECTS RESUME HOBBIES BIRDS

Outside of work and technical projects, I have many hobbies including backpacking, running, and quilting. This page highlights a few of the crafts I've made over the years. My Grandmother taught me how to sew and inspired my love of fiber arts. A friend taught me how to use a long arm sewing machine so I was able to long-arm quilt some of my quilts. In 2016 I explored wood turning to make bowls. I've also taken a variety of glass classes ranging from glass blowing to glass fusing to screen printing on glass.
